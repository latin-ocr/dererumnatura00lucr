Now Martial, addressing his fourth book, advises it to gain
the approval of Apollinaris, and tells it, if condemned, to be
off to the trunk-makers’ :

si damnaverit, ad salariorum
curras scrinia protinus licebitss.

The words are almost exactly those of Catullus 3 the mean-
ing is entirely different. There can be no stronger proof of
ingrained familiarity than such unconscious imitation.
